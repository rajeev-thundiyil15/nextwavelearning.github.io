Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

What age groups do you teach?

We offer classes for children aged 7-15, divided into appropriate age groups to ensure the best learning experience.

Do you provide the equipment?

Yes, all necessary equipment and materials are provided during the classes. Students can also purchase their own kits if they wish to practice at home.

How large are the classes?

We maintain small class sizes with a maximum of 8 students per instructor to ensure personalized attention.
