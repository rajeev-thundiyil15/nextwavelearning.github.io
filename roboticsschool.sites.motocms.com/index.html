WHAT WE DO

About RoboTech.

We offer structured and interesting courses designed by IIT-IIM and PhD professors.

If you are looking for some activities to engage your child from 6 years old, we have a solution. Robotics is an exciting hobby that can influence your child's future career choices. Robotics develops thinking, fine motor skills and logic, teaches children to understand the technology, and generally increases their creativity.

We've designed fun, practice-filled activities for your kids aged 6 to 16. Sign up for a free trial lesson, and your kid will love robotics!

FAQ

Want to Know More?

Feel free to contact us to get any details or book a free lesson for your child! 

  • What is the right age to join the courses?

    Children as young as 6 years old can start learning how to build robots and program them. 

  • What do parents need to bring for the class ?

    Actually, nothing. All equipment is provided by our school (including tablets/laptops). 

  • What is the class size and configuration ?

    The class will have maximum of 12 kids. Children work in small teams to build and code robots.

  • Can the kids keep the robots they've made?

    No, because we create a new robot each lesson. Every class, kids will learn to build new robots with additional features.

  • Do kids need experience with robotics?

    No prior experience or knowledge of robotics/programming is required.
